Reflecting On My Journey Through This Project

This project has truly been a game-changer in preparing me to tackle real-world data challenges across various industries. From mastering the traditional modeling techniques like ARIMA and SARIMA to diving into advanced techniques such as ARIMAX, SARIMAX, VAR, and even delving into deep learning with LSTM and GRU models, I’ve felt empowered to handle complex data scenarios with confidence.

Beyond just technical skills, this learning journey has deepened my understanding of the importance of data visualization, model interpretation, critical thinking in the analysis process and effectively communicating findings to stakeholders. As I look ahead, I’m certain that the lessons learned here will continue to guide me in making meaningful contributions to real-world temporal data challenges.

Lastly, I am deeply grateful to Dr. Purna for her invaluable expertise and mentorship which has been instrumental in shaping my learning journey and preparing me for the success of this project and course.
